English Grammar Practice

Welcome to English Grammar Practice

This page is designed to provide basic grounding in English grammar for students who have enrolled in Humber College's TESL program. The information provided here is a simplified version of the complex subject of English grammar, carefully designed to prepare you for the program.


While we've strived to cover the basics, English grammar has many facets and nuances that will be explored in more depth during your course. This page should be seen as a solid stepping stone that will help you start off on the right foot in your studies.


If you're just starting out in English grammar or would like to refresh your basics before diving into the program, the sections below will be a great starting point.

  • Adjectives
